Venue

Middlesex University Mauritius, located in the vibrant and picturesque touristic location of Flic en Flac. Flic en Flac is a beautiful location along the west coast of Mauritius. With its reputation as a popular tourist destination, Flic en Flac provides a wealth of opportunities for exquisite multi-cultural experiences.

It has quickly gained the reputation of a top and attractive holiday destination. Flic en Flac offers a pleasant and charming setting with beautiful white sand beaches and stunning blue lagoons. The IE2023 conference venue offers a diverse selection of top-tier accommodations, including charming guesthouses and luxury resorts, ensuring that every attendee can experience a comfortable and enjoyable stay. Info about hotels in the region of the conference venue (Flic-en-Flac) can be found here [1] [2].
An accommodation package for foreign visiting students has also been proposed. More details on this can be found here.

Airport Nearby

Sir Seewoosagur Ramgoolam International Airport (22 miles southeast)

Transportation Information

  • There is a wide range of accommodation options available in the region of Flic-en-Flac.
  • The most convenient mode of transportation from the airport to Flic-en-Flac is by car.
  • It is advisable to check with your hotel if they provide a complimentary airport taxi service, as many hotels offer this as part of their package.
  • Taxi fares from the airport to your hotel in Flic-en-Flac may vary between Rs1500 to Rs2000.
  • Additionally, car rental services are available at the airport. You can make a reservation by visiting this link.
