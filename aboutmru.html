About Mauritius

You will be enchanted by Mauritius, which will elevate your spirits and make you feel special. Every visitor receives individualized service. Every encounter is an opportunity to discover a pleasant face. Behind each smile is the promise of a unique stay. The contrast of a multiplicity of colours and flavours, the island, located in its turquoise water, is a sanctuary of serenity and tranquillity. The fundamental beauty of Mauritius, a melting pot where the past and present are harmoniously merged, would compel you to visit its shores often.
