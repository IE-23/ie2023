Awards

The following awards will be presented at the IE 2023 Conference:

  • Best Full Paper Award
  • Best Short Paper Award
  • Beatrice WORSLEY award to the best Female Researcher
  • Best Presentation Awards for a Full Paper Presentation
    • by a female presenter
    • by a male presenter

This twin presentation award has been set up to encourage equal visibility of female and male researchers. The awards will be allocated to the whole co-authors of the presented paper. The attribution will be decided by a jury of people of equally balanced gender. The Beatrice Worsley Prize to Best Female Researcher is to encourage females innovators in this field.

Beatrice WORSLEY award will be accompanied with a certificate and a free 12-month online subscription to JAISE provided by our sponsor IOS Press.

Each remaining award will be accompanied with a certificate and a free 3-month online subscription to JAISE provided by our sponsor IOS Press.

More prizes are being considered, and could be announced at a later date.
